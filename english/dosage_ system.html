Dosage system

The dyehouse’s modern dosage unit provides the dyeing and/or washing process with the right doses of dyestuffs and auxiliaries at the right time.

Not surprisingly, the dosage unit is an indispensable and vital link in the dyeing process; it reduces the chances of colour differences in large lots to a minimum.
