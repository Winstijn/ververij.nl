Laboratory

In the lab, different samples and recipes are made and used on different textiles. 

These recipes can be made for all cellulose fibres such as cotton, viscose and linen, direct or reactive wool, silk, acetate and polyamide.

Samples are also made based on colour samples from the client or based on the pantone system (tpx-tc-tp). 

Because the dyehouse has its own laboratory, we are not dependent on a third party.
